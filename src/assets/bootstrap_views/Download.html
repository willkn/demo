Our Proposals for The Avenue, Campsall

Together Housing are proposing to build 20 family homes on land formally known as the Campsall Club.  The site has been derelict for several years and the former club has fallen into disrepair, becoming a focus of antisocial behaviour.  
 
The homes we are planning will go some way to address the housing shortage in Doncaster, we have liaised with DMBC to understand the housing need in the area and our proposals for a mix of 2 and 3 bedroomed family homes address this.  We are committed to delivering affordable homes for the benifit of the community, our proposals arew to provide homes for affordable rent.

Together Housing are seeking to engage with the community and obtain your feedback on our proposals prior to finalising our design and submitting for Planning Approval.  If granted we will look to complete the construction of the homes within approximately 1 year of starting construction works.

Family Homes

We propose to build 20 affordable, family  homes for both Affordable Rent and Shared Ownership.

Energy Efficient

Our homes will be energy efficient, built to exceed the requirements of the current Building Regulations.

Quality Design

The homes will be designed and constructed to a high standard with a focus on family living.
